FAQ

Common questions

Is Terra a law firm?

No. Terra is an information and case-management platform. Premium members can choose to connect with independent, licensed immigration attorneys for legal advice and representation.

How does Terra know which policy changes affect me?

Your case profile — visa category, filing stage, and jurisdiction — is matched against policy updates as they're published, so you only get alerts relevant to your situation.

Can I self-file without a lawyer?

Yes. Terra's Free and Premium tiers both include full case tracking, timeline predictions, and application/interview prep built for self-filers.

What kinds of cases does Terra support?

Terra supports a wide range of case types, including family- and employment-based green cards, work visas, naturalization, and asylum cases.
